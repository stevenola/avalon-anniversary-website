Steve

Founder & President

My first job after college was working for a department store chain where I learned about operations, buying and store management. My experience was often trial by fire which gave me plenty of opportunities to learn from my mistakes.

Over time I realized most retailers face the challenges I had, but there were not a lot of sources for help. That led me to start Avalon in 1996 where I learned how small businesses should work. From these experiences I learned the skills I use to help our clients grow their business and hopefully avoid some of the mistakes I made.

Katie Barton

Senior Consultant

Katie Barton is an accomplished member of the Avalon team with an expertise in the fashion industry. She worked with California based contemporary designer Cynthia Vincent for seven years expanding the brand across the globe. Katie launched her New York showroom and managed department store business working closely with buyers and store managers.

She was responsible for all regional markets as well as promoting the brand during fashion week. In addition to her wholesale experience, Katie has worked in small business and corporate retail. Katie is a graduate of Louisiana State University with a Bachelor of Arts in Marketing.

Kelly Grace

Retail Consultant

With over 10 years of experience in education and business management and a love for fashion, Kelly is a dedicated and enthusiastic team player who takes great pride in her work. Her retail experience started early on in her late teen years, working at a few New Orleans well-known women's boutiques during the summers and on the weekends.

After graduating from the University of Georgia in 2009, Kelly spent three years in Washington, D.C., where she taught in the public school system. In 2012, Kelly moved home to New Orleans to continue her teaching career. After the arrival of her twin boys, Kelly shifted her focus to business management, a role in which she quickly flourished. In 2022, she co-founded and launched Get Organized, a home organization company.
