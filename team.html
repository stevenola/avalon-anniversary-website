Steve

Founder & President

My first job after college was working for a department store chain where I learned about operations, buying and store management. From there I gained further retail and wholesale experience

Over time I realized most retailers face the challenges I had, but there were not a lot of sources for help. That led me to start Avalon in 1996 where I learned how small businesses should work. From these experiences I learned the skills I use to help our clients grow their businesses and hopefully avoid some of the mistakes I made.

Katie Barton

Retail Consultant

Katie Barton is an accomplished member of the Avalon team with an expertise in the fashion industry. She worked with California based contemporary designer Cynthia Vincent for seven years expanding the brand across the globe. Katie launched her New York showroom and managed department store business working closely with buyers and store managers.

She was responsible for all regional markets as well as promoting the brand during fashion week. In addition to her wholesale experience, Katie has worked in small business and corporate retail. Katie is a graduate of Louisiana State University with a Bachelor of Arts in Marketing.

Kelly Grace

Retail Consultant

Kelly brings a rare combination of hands-on boutique experience and sharp analytical thinking to her work at Avalon. Her love of fashion and retail began in her teen years working at well-known women's boutiques in New Orleans — an experience that gave her a genuine understanding of what it feels like to be on the floor, managing inventory, and serving customers. She carries that perspective into every client relationship, approaching each boutique's challenges with both empathy and precision.

After graduating from the University of Georgia with a Bachelor of Arts in Marketing, Kelly built a career grounded in education and business management before joining the Avalon team three years ago. Known for her exceptional attention to detail and focused approach, Kelly helps boutique owners make sense of their numbers, sharpen their buying strategies, and build the kind of organized, sustainable businesses they set out to create. Her clients consistently describe her as thorough, responsive, and genuinely invested in their success.
